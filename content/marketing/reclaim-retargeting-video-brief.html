2. How to film (simple notes)

  • Length: about 20–30 seconds each. Short is better.
  • Phone orientation: vertical (upright) if you can—works best for Instagram/Facebook.
  • Look: same nice backdrop you already use; bright and calm.
  • Who talks: mostly Lee Anne. Sara stands with her, warm and natural. On Video B, Sara may open with one line if she likes.
  • First few seconds: look at the camera and start with the opening line right away (no long hello).
  • Words on screen: many people watch without sound—we’ll add simple captions from the “On-screen text” column.
  • How to end: “Tap Learn More and save your intro spot.”
  • Price (please keep accurate): intro $597 · usually $999 · first 10 women. No extra discounts.
  • Optional gentle line (only if it feels natural on one take): “If you’re not ready to join yet, there’s also a free fit call on the page.”

6. Helpful reminders

Please do

  • Keep the main invite about joining R.E.C.L.A.I.M.
  • Name the program and six weeks of private coaching
  • Mention $597 vs $999 and first 10 spots
  • End with “save your intro spot” / Learn More

  • Match the offer on the website

Please skip

  • Pushing another free PDF download
  • Making the free call the whole message
  • Hiding the price
  • Promising medical or guaranteed weight results
  • Any price lower than $597 intro
  • Long hellos before the first real line
