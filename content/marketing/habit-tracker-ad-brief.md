# Habit Tracker Ad Landing Brief

## Landing URL (use in Meta)

`https://mindandbodyresetcoach.com/habit-tracker-invite`

Primary CTA opens the live app: `/habit-tracker`  
Secondary: sign in to sync  

Chat is hidden on this page.

## What the product actually is

| Tab | Path | Features |
|-----|------|----------|
| Dashboard | `/habit-tracker` | Daily habits, custom habits, notes, challenges, push, guest or account |
| Macros | `/habit-tracker/calories` | Meals + calories/protein/carbs/fat/fiber, AI food photo estimate (signed-in) |
| Fitness | `/habit-tracker/fitness` | Workout log + video library (signed-in for full log) |

## Campaign tips

- Objective: Traffic or App-style engagement (landing views / clicks)
- Optimize for ViewContent “Habit Tracker Invite Landing” or link clicks
- Creative: phone mock / dashboard OG image (`/og-habit-tracker.jpg`)
- Audience: midlife women, wellness, habit interest; retarget site visitors
- Don’t pitch RECLAIM hard on this LP — free tool first

## Sample ad angles

1. “One free tracker for habits, meals & workouts — built for women 40+”  
2. “Tired of three apps? Habits + macros + fitness in one free place”  
3. “Start free. Optional account to sync. No credit card.”
