## 8. Ad brief template (copy once per campaign)

| Line | Fill in |
|------|---------|
| **Advertising objective** | 1–2 sentences + measurable outcome + time frame |
| **Target** | One person caricature (age, evening struggle, what she tried) |
| **Insight** | Her words: mind + heart (e.g. “I know what to do by day; 9pm I lose myself”) |
| **Promise** | How we solve that (one clear promise) |
| **Support** | Reasons to believe (sessions, reviews, price, certs) — bullets, ordered |
| **Tone** | 2–3 words only (e.g. *calm, direct, hopeful*) |
| **Assignment** | Channel + URL (e.g. Meta RT → reclaim-invite) |

**Storytelling (SUCCESs):** Simple · Unexpected · Concrete · Credible · Emotional · Story.  
**Proof on file:** Chrissy O., Nicole L., Sherylee (use in ads + LPs).
